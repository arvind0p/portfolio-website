About Me

I am a motivated and results-oriented Marketing Executive based in Lucknow, India, with a Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Marketing. My academic background has provided me with a strong understanding of marketing strategy, sales management, consumer behavior, and business operations.

Professionally, I have gained hands-on experience in sales operations, lead generation, customer relationship management, and business development. I have worked closely with clients to understand their requirements, identify growth opportunities, and deliver solutions that align with organizational objectives.

During my professional journey, I have been actively involved in prospecting, negotiating, and closing deals, as well as supporting promotional campaigns and exhibitions to enhance brand visibility. I believe in maintaining long-term relationships with clients by providing consistent value and professional service.

I strongly believe in a customer-centric and data-driven approach to marketing. I enjoy analyzing market trends, understanding customer needs, and converting insights into actionable marketing strategies that contribute to measurable business outcomes.

I am a quick learner, adaptable to new environments, and comfortable working in fast-paced, performance-driven settings. My long-term goal is to grow into leadership roles where I can contribute strategically to organizational growth, mentor teams, and drive sustainable business success.
